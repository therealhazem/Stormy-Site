Curved Classic

The Princess is a timeless classic, with its smooth curved edges that give this design a resort look and feel ideal for any family backyard.

Features

Smooth curved design
Convenient steps and seating ledge along the length of the pool
Child safety ledge around the perimeter
Gradual depth for wading and learning to swim
Durability, strength and resilience tested fibreglass pool shell
Available as a Mineral Water Pool or Salt Water Pool
Freestanding options available on Princess 9m and below
